# Steadybee — Vision

## One line
Steadybee is the honest, affordable, once-a-year retirement check-up that tells people with real
money whether they're actually on track — and what's quietly putting them at risk.

## The problem
Millions of people over 45 share the same private worry: *"I've saved some money… but is it
enough, and am I taking a stupid risk without realising it?"*

They are stuck in a gap:
- **Financial advisers** cost ~£1–5k/year and many people don't trust them or feel "too small."
- **Free calculators** (Vanguard, HL, pension providers) are generic, reassuring, and tell you
  what you want to hear because *you* supply the optimistic numbers.
- **Their broker dashboard** shows today's balance but says nothing about whether they'll be okay.

So they fly blind. The result is chronic, low-grade financial anxiety and avoidable mistakes made
right at the finish line of their working life.

## Who it's for
Self-directed UK investors, broadly **age 45–70 with £150k+** across ISAs and pensions, who manage
their own money (no adviser, or burned by one) and who **fear an expensive mistake near retirement
more than they crave maximum upside.**

Two segments, served by one connected product:
- **Primary — anxious near-retirees (52–70):** buy the *future* ("will my money last?").
- **Secondary — analytical mid-career DIY (40–52):** buy the *present-tense* diagnostics ("what's
  hidden in what I hold?").

**Not for:** under-35s, day traders, crypto/FOMO investors, index purists who'll never look again,
or pots under ~£20k. We don't chase them and won't distort the product to win them.

## What Steadybee is
An **annual independent retirement second opinion**, delivered as a clear, professional report,
with a free personalised check-up as the front door. Its one unique thing: it **connects today's
portfolio to your future retirement outcome** — *your hidden concentration today → how it behaves
in a market shock → what that does to your retirement date and the odds your money lasts.*

Nobody else joins those two halves: brokers stop at today, calculators stop at the future.

## What Steadybee is NOT
- **Not regulated financial or tax advice.** We show you the picture and the risks; you make the
  decisions. We never say "buy this fund."
- **Not a product seller.** No funds, no commissions, no kickbacks — so we have no reason to tell
  you anything but the truth. Independence is the whole point.
- **Not a trading app, not a daily-engagement habit app, not a native mobile app.**

## Mission
Replace the quiet dread of "I don't know where I'm heading" with calm, honest clarity — and make
genuinely good retirement insight affordable to ordinary people, not just the wealthy with advisers.

## Brand & voice — "approachable expertise"
The bee is deliberate: **steady, industrious, trustworthy.** Steadybee is the worker that keeps
*your* hive in order. We use the warmth of the brand to lower the intimidation of finance — but the
*content* is rigorous, sourced, and honest. The tone is **warm but credible; friendly but never
flippant.** A reassuring expert, not a cartoon. Light hive/honey/nest-egg motifs are fine; baby-talk
is not — our users are trusting us with their life savings.

## Honest framing of scale
This is a **focused, high-margin, solo-viable business** — realistic ceiling roughly £250k–£2M
annual revenue. It is *not* a venture-scale company, and that is completely fine: it earns real
money by solving a real, painful problem honestly, for an audience that is badly underserved.
